Sudoku Online
Welcome to Sudoku Online! There are 110 (working on making this figure 1000) Sudoku Puzzles here for you to solve, and you can access all of them using the navigation menu to your left.
Your progress on each level will be saved so feel free to switch between levels as you like, and if you have not toggled with your browser's content settings to restrict webpages' access to local data (if you're not sure what this means, think cookies), your progress will also still be saved even after you close this window. Just remember to open it back up in the same browser!
If you are still wondering about what sudoku is, no worries! I have included some easy-to-understand instructions that you can make quick references to as you attempt any of the levels. Just look for the question mark icon.
That's all for my greetings here. Hope you enjoy tackling those puzzles!

Actions
Drag
To fill a sudoku tile with a number, drag any of the number pieces on the panel to the right of the game board on to an empty tile. You may replace a used number piece by dropping an unused number piece on it.
Double Click
To remove a number from a sudoku tile, double click on the number piece.
Objects
Given Numbers
Given numbers are gray colored, and cannot be altered.
Number Pieces
Number pieces have green backgrounds and can be placed on sudoku tiles not occupied by a given number. A counter for the remaining number of times you can use a number piece is displayed to the right of each of the number pieces.
